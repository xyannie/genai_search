Crypto
What is the product? (TLDR)
With MoneyLion crypto, you can easily buy and sell Bitcoin, Ethereum, Litecoin, and Bitcoin Cash whenever you want. 

Product features:
Round Ups: Spend on your RoarMoney or any existing debit or credit card and invest your spare change into Bitcoin
Auto Invest: Leverage dollar cost averaging by automatically investing in crypto on your custom schedule.
MoneyLion Academy: Learn all about crypto in the app

Why are we building this product/feature? 
Make investing in crypto easy and accessible for anyone that wants to dip their toes into popular cryptocurrencies
Help MoneyLion be the one-stop destination for all financial needs

How does it work? 
Download the MoneyLion app
Open a RoarMoney account
Open your crypto account
Fund your RoarMoney account
Invest in Bitcoin, Ethereum, Litecoin, or Bitcoin Cash
